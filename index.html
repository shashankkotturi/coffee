COffEE (Context Offloading on the Edge for Enterprises) keeps AI agents sharp, scalable, and cost-effective. By only loading the context they need, when they need it, we cut waste by up to 90% — while boosting accuracy and efficiency.

Why COffEE?

Most AI agents overload themselves — dragging every tool, rule, and prompt into memory, even for trivial tasks. The result: wasted tokens, reduced context for real work, and ballooning costs.

COffEE flips this model: it dynamically loads only what’s relevant. That means scalability (thousands of tools supported), accuracy (longer conversations stay consistent), and efficiency (up to 90% fewer wasted tokens).

About COffEE

Coffee is a standalone framework designed for enterprises that want AI that scales without exploding costs. Inspired by how humans refuel with coffee, it gives AI agents the same boost — but in the form of context management.
